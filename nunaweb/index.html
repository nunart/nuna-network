Executive Summary

Nuna Network is a sovereign blockchain based on Cosmos, developed using the Rollkit framework. Its mission is to revolutionize the field of art and culture through blockchain technology, providing an accessible and secure ecosystem that allows for the preservation, funding, and monetization of art in all its forms.

Introduction

With the advancement of digitalization, art is entering an era where trust and innovation are essential. Nuna Network responds to this need through a sovereign blockchain platform that allows artists and communities to actively participate in the creation, monetization, and preservation of art.

Mission and Vision

• Mission: To democratize access to art and culture through blockchain technology, providing artists worldwide with a secure and collaborative platform to share and monetize their work.

• Vision: To become the main bridge between art and blockchain technology, building a global community committed to the preservation and promotion of art and culture in a decentralized environment.

Core Technology and Benefits of Using Rollkit

Nuna Network is built on the Cosmos blockchain, leveraging Rollkit to ensure its sovereignty without compromising connection to the Cosmos ecosystem.

  • Total Sovereignty

    Nuna Network operates autonomously, allowing the community of artists and collectors to establish their own rules and governance policies.

  • Flexibility and Modularity

    Rollkit facilitates customization, allowing interoperability with Cosmos IBC and access to all tools from the Cosmos SDK.

  • Scalability and Cost Efficiency

    The sovereign structure of Nuna eliminates the need for external validators, reducing costs and allowing Nuna to scale rapidly.

  • Direct Interoperability

    Nuna Network easily connects with other blockchains within the Cosmos ecosystem.

Project Components

1. Development of Digital Identity for Artists: Nuna Network provides each artist with a Decentralized Digital Identity (DID) that ensures each artwork is linked to its creator.

2. Community Funding for Artistic Projects: Nuna Network enables token holders to fund artistic projects of their choice.

3. Artistic Residencies and Exhibitions in the Metaverse: Nuna offers artistic residencies in the metaverse.

4. NFT Marketplace for Digital Art and Cultural Heritage: Nuna Network enables the tokenization and sale of NFTs that include visual art, poetry, music, and more.

5. Collection and Preservation of Urban and Community Art: The network digitizes and monetizes urban and community art.

Nuna Network Roadmap

Why Rollkit for Nuna Network

Rollkit addresses typical challenges in implementing smart contracts and new Layer 1 chains by introducing rollup chains, which offer a scalable and efficient solution.

Advantages of Rollkit for Nuna Network

1. Shared Security: Rollups implemented with Rollkit inherit their security from larger and more robust consensus layers, providing enhanced security and trust.

2. Cost Optimization: Reducing costs by connecting with the base chain of Cosmos, allowing for efficient transaction processing and lower fees.

3. Enhanced Performance: Rollkit enables faster transaction processing and increased throughput, making it ideal for applications that require high performance.

4. Simplified Development: Developers can build on top of Rollkit without needing to reinvent the wheel, as it provides a flexible framework for creating customized solutions.
